Biography

Rubén Hernández-Murillo is a senior policy economist at the Federal Reserve Bank of Cleveland. He received a MA and a PhD in economics from the University of Rochester in 1998 and 2001, respectively, and a BA in economics from ITAM (Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México) in 1995. His research interests include Public Finance, Public Economics, Real Estate Economics, Regional and Urban Economics, Industrial Organization, and Political Economy.
