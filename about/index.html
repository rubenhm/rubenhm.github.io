About

My name is Rubén Hernández-Murillo.

I am a economist, currently at the Research Division of the Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis.

My research interests include regional and urban economics, public economics and public finance, and industrial organization.

I attended ITAM in Mexico City and the University of Rochester in Rochester, NY.

In this website I publicize my research and publications. The main input for this effort is my bibliography file in BibTeX format.

The source files for this website are hosted in GitHub.
